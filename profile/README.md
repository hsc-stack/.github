## Our Story

It started small — a friend and I uploading our practical notebooks to Google Drive and sharing them around school so our batchmates wouldn't have to struggle for resources.

Then we thought: why stop at practicals?

**HSC Stack** grew out of that idea — a single place where HSC students can find notes, suggestions, and resources for every subject, organized and free for anyone who needs them.

## What This Is

This isn't just our project — it belongs to the whole batch, and beyond. It's built by students, for students, so that good resources aren't locked away or hard to find.

Whether you're looking for notes, or you have something worth sharing yourself, HSC Stack is here for that.
